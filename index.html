Key Features

Easy Waste Collection Scheduling

Effortless Scheduling at Your Fingertips: Say goodbye to complicated waste collection arrangements. Our intuitive platform allows you to schedule pickups in just a few clicks. Simply select your preferred date and time, and we'll connect you with a reliable local collector.

Recurring Scheduling Options: Set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly pickups to fit your needs, ensuring consistent and hassle-free waste management.

Real-Time Availability: View collector availability in real-time, allowing you to choose the most convenient pickup slot.

Automated Reminders: Receive timely notifications and reminders to ensure you never miss a collection.

Secure Payment Options

Peace of Mind with Secure Transactions: Your financial security is our top priority. We offer a range of secure payment options, including M-Pesa and other trusted methods.

Encrypted Payment Processing: All payment transactions are encrypted using industry-standard security protocols, safeguarding your sensitive data.

Transparent Billing: Receive clear and detailed invoices for every transaction, providing full transparency and accountability.

Payment History Tracking: Easily access your payment history and manage your subscriptions through your secure client portal.

Location-Based Collector Matching

Find Local Collectors, Fast: We leverage advanced location technology to connect you with verified waste collectors in your immediate vicinity.

Support Your Community: By choosing local collectors, you're not only ensuring efficient service but also supporting your local economy.

Optimized Routing: Our system optimizes collection routes, minimizing travel time and reducing environmental impact.

Collector Profiles: Browse detailed collector profiles, including their service areas, ratings, and reviews, to make informed decisions.

Interactive Map Integration: View nearby garbage collectors on an interactive map, and easily select the one that best suits your needs.
